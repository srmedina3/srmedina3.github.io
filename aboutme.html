About Me

My name is Samantha Medina. I am a Mass Communications student with an emphasis in integrated communications and I am currently on the CSU-Pueblo dance team. My interests and passion are everything in the beauty industry. Whether it comes to skin care, makeup, perfume, hair care, and overall self care, I love to research and try new products that work good for me in my everyday life.

I look for quality, price, performance, and durability in each product that I use. Is it worth the money? Does it give quality results? How well does it perform? How long can this last me? I have grown up as a dancer. I danced at my local dance studio for 14 years. I was on the dance team in high school all four years and now I am currently in my third year as a Wolfpack dancer for CSU-Pueblo. I have always loved getting my hair and makeup all done up for dance performances and recitals. I have used so many products throughout the years that have made me feel my best and I know work best for me.

Throughout the years my confidence has grown. Of course, I have had my insecurities but performing in front of crowds while feeling my best is what helped me. But I have also found confidence in myself without being all done up. Being comfortable in my own skin and embracing myself has helped so much.

I started mostly in high school with discovering different products. As my skin was changing I wanted to try different things that could make my skin look and feel better. I tried different skin care treatments and regimens. I then went to makeup for coverage. After I had some control of my skin with products, I wanted to find what could help my hair. I needed something to help with dryness, length, and overall health with my hair. I wanted to take care of myself and body but at the same time feel my best.

I love when I find a new product and I get to recommend it to someone. Knowing a product works good for me, makes me want to share with others. I have friends and family who come to me for recommendations. I learn from watching videos and others doing makeup, skin care and even hair care.

The beauty industry is so big nowadays. Stores like Ulta and Sephora are full of everything and anything beauty. I love what the stores stand for of expressing yourself and feeling confident. The beauty industry has made me feel my best in every way.
